Turning Raw Footage Into Stories

✦ First-Time Freelance Editor — Now Open for Projects

I'm Hariharan R, a passionate video editor based in Chennai. I specialise in creating high-quality edits that capture attention and tell stories that resonate — whether it's a reel, a YouTube video, or a brand film. I'm currently stepping into freelance editing for the first time, bringing fresh energy, dedication, and a genuine commitment to delivering work I'm proud of.

With hands-on editing experience and a keen eye for pacing, visual flow, I treat every client project as an opportunity to prove what I can do. If you're looking for an editor who's hungry, attentive, and fully invested — I'd love to work with you.
